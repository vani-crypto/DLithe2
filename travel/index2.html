Majuli, Assam

Nestled in the Brahmaputra River, Majuli is the world's largest river island. Renowned for its vibrant culture and scenic landscapes, it offers a serene escape into nature.

Hampi, Karnataka

Once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire, Hampi is dotted with impressive ruins and temples. Its unique boulder-strewn landscape adds to its historical charm.

Spiti Valley, Himachal Pradesh

Known for its rugged terrain and ancient monasteries, Spiti Valley offers a glimpse into Tibetan culture. The stark beauty of its high-altitude desert landscape is mesmerizing. .
